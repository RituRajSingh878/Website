About the Society

Vision

The Mathematics and Computing Society of IIT BHU (MACS) is a non-profit student organization,under the patronage of the Department of Mathematical Sciences IIT BHU,aimed at promoting research and technical skills among the students.It will provide a plateform for students to develope their curricular and extra-curricular skills and opportunity to elucidate their ideas and thougths beyond boundaries.It will act as a collaborative and symbiotic linkage among the various units of the Department and it's Alumni.

About IIT BHU


The Indian Institute of Technology BHU (abbreviated IITB or IIT BHU) is a public engineering institution located in Varanasi, India. In global rankings, IIT BHU has emerged as India’s top university.
